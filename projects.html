Vyond — Continuous User Testing

User Research · Usability Testing · Knowledge Sharing

Ran 100+ moderated and unmoderated remote usability tests on the platform — testing whether users could discover and complete core tasks, and validating parity with the legacy app across international markets. Evangelised learnings company-wide to build a user-centred culture across product and engineering.
