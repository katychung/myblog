Hello — I’m Katy.

I’m a UX designer with 10 years experience across startups, big tech and agencies — London, Hong Kong, Melbourne and now Tokyo.

I design end-to-end, bridging design and engineering — demystifying what it takes to get from concept to something that actually ships.

Born and raised in the UK, I have a BSc in Interactive Media Design. I’ve worked with multidisciplinary teams across the full product lifecycle, from discovery through delivery.

I speak English natively, Cantonese fluently and Japanese conversationally (JLPT N2).
